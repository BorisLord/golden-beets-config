# gbc junk-tag patterns -- one case-insensitive regex per line ('#' comments + blank lines ignored).
# Read FRESH each run by the `junk` pass; any VALUE of comments / grouping / composer / lyricist / lyrics /
# encoder / albumdisambig that MATCHES a line gets blanked. Identity tags (title/artist/album/albumartist) are
# NEVER touched. Keep patterns SPECIFIC -- a too-broad pattern (e.g. a bare '@') would nuke legit text such as
# "Bowie @ the BBC". Add your own here, or in $BEETSDIR/junk_patterns.txt (also read each run).

# --- URLs ---
https?://\S+
www\.\S+

# --- bare rip/leak-site domains (no scheme): rnbxclusive.se, sinmasmp3.tk, soulection.com, collection.by.ru ---
\b[\w-]{2,}\.(com|net|org|info|biz|se|ru|tk|cc|to|me|us)\b

# --- rip / encode signatures (greedy to end-of-line: the whole "ripped/encoded by X" phrase is junk) ---
\bripped\s+(by|from)\b.*
\bencoded\s+(by|for)\b.*
\bmp3\s*producer\b.*
easy cd-?da extractor.*

# --- scene / uploader decoration tags (two ':: ' groups + their '.' decoration, or '@@@', or '@+') ---
\.*::.*::\.*
@@@
@\+

# --- leaked raw ID3 fields dumped into the comment (e.g. "YEAR: 1968 ID3G: 115") ---
\bID3G\b.*
\bYEAR:\s*\d{4}\b

# --- named leak sites (the bare-domain rule above also catches these; listed for sigs without a TLD) ---
rnbxclusive
hotreleases
file24ever
verycd
datpiff
livemixtap
hotnewhiphop
